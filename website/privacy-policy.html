Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gyango ("we", "us", "our") handles personal data for GyanGo (package: ai.gyango). GyanGo is an offline-first, on-device AI tutor built for kids and curious learners.

1. Core privacy approach

We design GyanGo to work offline-first and process learning interactions on the device wherever possible. We aim to minimize data collection and give users clear control over app permissions and data choices.

2. Permissions we request

GyanGo may request certain Android permissions to enable specific features:

  • Microphone (android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO): used only when the user chooses voice interaction.
  • Camera or photos: used only when the user intentionally captures or selects an image for learning features.

If you do not grant these permissions, those optional features may not work, but core app functions may still be available.

3. Camera and photo usage

GyanGo does not continuously access camera or photos. Camera/photo access happens only on user action, such as tapping an upload or capture button for an image-based learning task.

4. Denying or revoking permissions

You can deny permissions when prompted and can later revoke them in Android Settings at any time. To change access, open your device settings, find GyanGo in app permissions, and update the permission choices.

5. What data we use and why

Depending on how you use the app, we may process:

  • Voice or text input you provide to generate tutoring responses.
  • Optional image input when you choose image understanding features.
  • Basic app diagnostics needed for reliability and security.

We use this information to provide educational responses, improve safety and reliability, and maintain app quality.

6. Data retention

We retain data only for as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, legal obligations, or security needs. Where practical, we limit retention and delete or de-identify data when it is no longer required.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited information with service providers who help us operate GyanGo (for example hosting or support tooling), under contractual safeguards. We may also share data when required by law or to protect users and our rights.

8. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data, including access controls and security-oriented engineering practices. No method of storage or transmission is fully risk-free, but we work to reduce risk.

9. Children and family use

GyanGo is intended for educational use with safety in mind. Parents and guardians should review app settings and supervise younger users. If you believe a child has provided personal data inappropriately, contact us and we will review and address the request.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves or legal requirements change. When we make material updates, we will revise the effective date and publish the latest policy on this page.

11. Contact us

Privacy questions: privacy@gyango.com
General support: support@gyango.com
