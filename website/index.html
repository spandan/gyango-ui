What GyanGo is

GyanGo is designed to support independent learning, homework help, and curiosity-driven exploration. It is built for children and learners of all ages who benefit from a calm, guided tutor experience.

Why offline-first matters

Offline-first design reduces dependency on always-on internet and helps keep sensitive interactions close to the learner's device. This approach supports privacy, resilience, and smoother learning in low-connectivity environments.

Safety for children

Child safety informs our product decisions. We aim for age-appropriate educational use, transparent data handling, and parental clarity around permissions and controls.

Voice, text, and optional image input

Learners can interact by speaking or typing. Optional image understanding is only used when a user chooses to provide a photo or camera input for a specific learning task.
